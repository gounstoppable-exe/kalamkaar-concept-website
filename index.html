ABOUT KALAMKAAR

We've a lot of things to tell you about ourselves but maybe you won't read that hundred page bio XD. Here is a key bio that defines our personality.

Kalamkaar is an independent and eclectic record label. It is India's fastest growing music platform, which houses and nurtures young names such as Rashmeet Kaur, KR$NA, Karma, Deep Kalsi, Brishav and Yunan. Kalamkaar embodies art, culture and an artist's freedom in the brand it has created. Kalamkaar has crosses 1,000,000+ YouTube subscribers which crowns it the tag of biggest hiphop label in India.Founded in 2018, Kalamkaar has a unique perspective on the music industry as it continues to focus on quality music and to work with artists whose work ethic, craftsmanship, talent and sounds are superior. It has helped launch the careers of new artists and has nurtured the careers of seasoned veterans. Founded back in 2018 by Raftaar and Ankit Khanna AKA AK Projects, Kalamkaar has came across a long way and is always striving to bring out the nation's talent to the audience.
